## What to AVOID

- **Paid link networks / link farms** — instant penalty risk per `backlink-analyzer` toxic-link rules
- **Reciprocal-link schemes** with unrelated sites
- **Comment spam** on blogs
- **Buying backlinks from Fiverr "100 backlinks for $5"** — toxic
- **PBN (private blog networks)** — manual action risk

## Recommended cadence

- **Week 1:** Tier 1 (5 listings, ~3 hours total)
- **Weeks 2–4:** Tier 2 (HVAC + energy authority)
- **Month 2:** Tier 3 (local chambers)
- **Month 3:** Tier 4 (start with 1 lead platform to test ROI)
- **Ongoing:** Tier 6 (content + outreach, 1 piece per month)
